About Me:

Hi, I’m Ben Eklund, a soon-to-be graduate from Sacramento State with a degree in Computer Science. Over the years, I’ve discovered a true passion for software engineering, where I get to combine my love for building things from scratch with solving complex problems. What excites me most is taking an idea, whether it’s a game mechanic or a large-scale system, and seeing it come to life through well-crafted code.

Currently, I’m working at Inductive Automation as a Software Support Engineer, where I specialize in troubleshooting and optimizing SCADA systems using the Ignition platform. My time in this role has not only sharpened my problem-solving skills but also exposed me to the high-pressure environments of real-time industrial systems. It’s helped me realize that no matter how challenging the problem, there's always a solution that can be designed and executed with efficiency.

I’m looking forward to transitioning into a dedicated software engineering role, where I can dive deeper into system architecture, optimization, and development at scale. In my free time, I explore personal projects, focusing on game development, backend services, and systems programming—fields where I can bring together my academic knowledge and industry experience to create impactful software.

If you're interested in seeing some of the projects I've worked on, feel free to check out my Projects page, or take a look at my GitHub to explore the code behind the work I do.
