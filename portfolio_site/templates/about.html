About Me:

I'm Benjamin Eklund, a soon-to-be graduate from Sacramento State with a degree in Computer Science. My academic journey has allowed me to build a strong foundation in software engineering, with a focus on game development, system architectures, and backend services. Through these experiences, I have developed a deep understanding of both object-oriented programming and efficient system design, laying the groundwork for my future career in software engineering.

This experience has sharpened my problem-solving skills and given me the ability to work under pressure, while reinforcing my technical understanding of large-scale industrial systems.

I’m passionate about transitioning into a software engineering role, where I can apply my knowledge and skills in developing efficient, scalable software solutions. I continuously expand my skill set by working on personal projects in game development, systems programming, and backend development, applying what I’ve learned both academically and in industry to create meaningful software.

Take a look at my work and explore my Projects page or visit my GitHub repositories, where I showcase a range of projects that reflect my passion for software engineering and development.
